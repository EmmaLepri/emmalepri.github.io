About me:

I obtained a Ph.D. in Mathematics at Università di Roma La Sapienza in May 2023, under the supervision of professor Marco Manetti. My Ph.D. thesis can be found here <\a>.
My research interests revolve around deformation theory, differential graded Lie algebras and L-infinity structures.
